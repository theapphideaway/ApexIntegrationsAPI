Modernizing Real Estate with Applied AI

Apex Integrations is the developer behind the Real Estate AI mobile platform. We provide brokerages and agents with secure, intelligent tools to automate contract generation, manage deal pipelines, and accelerate closings.

Core Platform

The Real Estate AI App

Built exclusively for modern agents, our iOS application streamlines the entire offer drafting process from your mobile device.

1

Voice-to-Contract AI

Dictate deal terms naturally. Our custom AI extraction engine parses your speech and automatically populates highly complex RE-21 residential offer forms in seconds.
2

DocuSign Integration

Fully integrated with DocuSign's API. Generate PDFs and dispatch them directly to buyers for legally binding e-signatures without ever leaving the app.
3

Agent Defaults & Profiles

Set your standard contract preferences once. The app intelligently merges your brokerage defaults with specific client details to prevent repetitive data entry.
4

Enterprise Security

Backed by a secure Django REST framework and AWS S3 storage. Role-based access ensures that client data remains strictly isolated and confidential.

About Apex Integrations LLC

Apex Integrations was founded to solve a distinct problem: real estate agents spend too much time tied to their desks drafting contracts, and not enough time in the field with their clients.

Operating out of Idaho Falls, ID, our team specializes in mobile security, cryptography, and artificial intelligence. We partner with established real estate brokerages to build custom technical solutions that eliminate friction in the transaction pipeline.
